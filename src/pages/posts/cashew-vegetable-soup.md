        - name: "corn"

        - name: "carrot"
          quantity: 1
          note: "diced"
        - name: "celery"
          quantity: 1
          unit: "stalk"
          note: "diced"
        - name: "cashews"
          quantity: 0.25
          unit: "cup"
        - name: "water"
---
Add ingredients to a 4L pot and fill the pot up with cold water.

Put pot on a stove on high.

Once the water boils, turn the stove down to low and simmer for 1.5 hours.
>The flavour does end up being sweet!
